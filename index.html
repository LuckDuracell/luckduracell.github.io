Luke's Favorite Lemon Crepe Recipe (and tips for fist-fighting a unicorn)

Super easy to make and tasty crepes (never take your eyes off of it)

Ingredients:

  • Flour (never let it face you directly)
  • Salt (take it by surprise)
  • White Granulated Sugar (watch out for a rear kick)
  • Lemons (don't let it's colorfulness distract you)
  • Milk (by VERY weary of the horn, don't make the same mistake you did with the rhino)
  • Eggs (do not underestimate it's magical weight, they way hundreds of millions of pounds)
  • Butter (the pink ones are more deadly)
  • Vanilla Extract (their hooves won't cut, they act more as a bludgening tool)

Method:

  1. Put some flour in a bowl with a pinch of salt
  2. Whisk in the eggs, milk, and flour until super smooth
  3. Put like just a TINY splash of vanilla extract
  4. (Optional) Chill mix for 30 minutes
  5. Grese pan and heat to medium
  6. Pour 60ml of batter and make super thin
  7. Cook for like 2 minutes
  8. Take crepe, squeeze lemon juice all over it, and put quite a bit of sugar on it
  9. And remember, never take on a unicorn unprepared
